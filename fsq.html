About Us

Foursquare Gospel Church Sabo, affectionately known as FGC Sabo, is not just a place of worship, but a sanctuary where the essence of joy and happiness permeates every aspect of our existence. At FGC Sabo, we are committed to providing unwavering support, guidance, and teachings to both new believers and seasoned Christians alike, empowering them with valuable insights on growth, faith, and a deepening relationship with Christ Jesus. Our beloved church is under the visionary leadership and pastoral care of the esteemed Reverend (Dr) Udo Agomuonso, a devoted servant of God who finds immense joy in diligently teaching, graciously blessing, and compassionately advising believers.

FGC Sabo, with its inception in 1994, was founded by the visionary Reverend Chinedu Emmanuel, who faithfully guided the church for a remarkable decade before gracefully transitioning the mantle to his esteemed associate, Reverend Tunde Fashoranti. Under Reverend Fashoranti's anointed leadership, the church thrived for an additional twelve fruitful years, fostering spiritual growth, nurturing faith, and cultivating a strong foundation for the congregation before handing over the baton to our current esteemed Senior Pastor. Throughout its rich history, FGC Sabo has consistently upheld its motto, 'Adding Value To Your Life....', a testament to the transformative impact experienced by over 200 devoted members who confidently bear witness to the life-changing value derived from their association with the church.

We extend a heartfelt invitation to you to join FGC Sabo and experience the blessings of relevant and life-transforming value tips that will inspire and uplift every facet of your existence. Come and embrace a community that resonates with the resounding chorus of Hallelujah, where your spiritual journey will be enriched, your faith nurtured, and your life abundantly blessed. Hallelujah indeed!.

Our Ministries

At FGC Sabo, we believe in the power of community and service, and our various ministries reflect this conviction. From the youngest members to the most seasoned believers, there is a place for everyone to belong, grow, and serve. Our ministries are designed to meet the diverse needs of our church family and the wider community, empowering individuals to deepen their faith, discover their unique gifts, and make a lasting impact for the glory of God. Whether you are seeking spiritual nourishment, fellowship, opportunities to serve, or a place to connect with others on a deeper level, we invite you to explore our ministries and find your place in the beautiful tapestry of God's work within our church. Together, let us grow in faith, inspire one another, and make a difference in the world around us. Welcome to a community where ministries thrive, hearts are transformed, and lives are forever changed. Here are our ministries specifically divided into 5:

  • Children

    • The children's ministry of our church is dedicated to providing a safe, nurturing, and engaging environment that fosters the spiritual growth and development of children aged 0 to 12. With a passion for cultivating young hearts and minds, we strive to create a vibrant space where children can embark on a transformative journey of faith, immersing themselves in the timeless teachings of the Bible and discovering the profound wisdom of biblical principles. Through dynamic and age-appropriate programs, interactive lessons, and meaningful activities, we aim to empower children to not only comprehend the message of God's love but also to apply it in their daily lives. Guided by caring and dedicated leaders, our children's ministry endeavors to nurture a deep, personal relationship between each child and Jesus Christ, providing them with a firm foundation upon which to build their faith and embrace the values of love, kindness, and compassion. Together, we embark on an exciting adventure of spiritual growth, equipping our young ones with the tools, knowledge, and support needed to navigate the challenges of life with unwavering faith and a steadfast connection to our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.

  • NxtGen

    • The teens ministry of our church is dedicated to providing a vibrant and inclusive space that fosters personal growth, spiritual development, and meaningful connections for young individuals between the ages of 13 and 19. Within this dynamic community, teenagers have the opportunity to connect with like-minded peers, engage in thought-provoking discussions, and build lasting friendships that support and encourage them on their journey of discovering God's purpose for their lives. Our dedicated team of leaders and mentors are committed to creating an environment that nurtures their faith, equips them with biblical wisdom, and empowers them to navigate the unique challenges of adolescence with grace and resilience. Through engaging programs, impactful worship experiences, relevant teachings, and intentional discipleship, we aim to guide and inspire our teens to deepen their relationship with God, embrace their identity as beloved children of God, and passionately pursue a life that honors and glorifies Him. Together, we embark on a transformative journey of faith, where young hearts are ignited, dreams are awakened, and lives are forever changed.

  • Youth

    • The youth ministry of our church is a vibrant and welcoming community specifically designed to cater to the needs of young adults between the ages of 18 and 35. Within this dynamic space, individuals are invited to connect with like-minded peers, explore their faith in a supportive environment, and cultivate authentic relationships that transcend age, background, and life experiences. Our dedicated team of mentors and leaders create engaging programs, thought-provoking discussions, and meaningful experiences that empower young adults to navigate the unique challenges and opportunities of adulthood while deepening their relationship with God. Through intentional discipleship, relevant teachings, and opportunities for service and outreach, we strive to equip and inspire the next generation to live out their faith boldly, pursue their God-given purpose passionately, and impact their communities and the world with the transformative power of the Gospel. Together, we embark on a journey of discovery, growth, and fellowship, where young adults are empowered to embrace their identity in Christ, develop their gifts and talents, and make a lasting impact for the Kingdom of God.

  • Women

    • The Women's Ministry at FGC Sabo is a vibrant and uplifting community, dedicated to providing a safe and nurturing space where women of all ages can gather, connect, and grow in their faith as they encourage, empower, and inspire one another on their unique spiritual journeys. Within this beautiful tapestry of sisterhood, women find solace, support, and strength as they navigate the joys and challenges of life. Through meaningful fellowship, transformative teachings, and intentional discipleship, the Women's Ministry fosters a deep sense of belonging and unity, cultivating authentic relationships that transcend age, background, and life experiences. This ministry recognizes and celebrates the inherent worth and immeasurable potential of every woman, encouraging them to embrace their true identity in Christ, discover their God-given gifts and talents, and confidently pursue their passions and dreams. Together, we embark on a journey of self-discovery, spiritual growth, and purposeful living, as we uplift and inspire one another to make a lasting impact in our families, communities, and the world. With hearts full of compassion and arms open wide, the Women's Ministry at FGC Sabo stands as a beacon of hope, love, and encouragement, empowering women to shine brightly and make a positive difference in the lives of those around them.

  • Men

    • The Men's Ministry at FGC Sabo is a transformative and empowering community that passionately equips and inspires men of all ages to embrace godly integrity, cultivate strong leadership skills within their families, and actively strive to make a positive and lasting impact in their communities. Through engaging fellowship, impactful teachings, and intentional discipleship, the Men's Ministry provides a supportive environment where men are encouraged to deepen their relationship with God, discover their unique strengths and gifts, and develop a strong sense of purpose and direction. This ministry recognizes the immense responsibility men have in their roles as husbands, fathers, and community leaders, and seeks to equip them with the tools and biblical wisdom necessary to navigate the complexities of modern life with wisdom, humility, and grace. By fostering a culture of accountability, authentic relationships, and transformative growth, the Men's Ministry at FGC Sabo empowers men to rise above societal pressures, embrace their identity as beloved sons of God, and boldly live out their faith in a way that positively impacts not only their own lives but also the lives of those around them. Together, we embark on a journey of personal growth, spiritual transformation, and impactful service, as we strive to be the men that God has called us to be, making a lasting difference in our families, communities, and beyond.
